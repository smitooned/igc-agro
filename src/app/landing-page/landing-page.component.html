Why Devgad mangoes?

The alphonso mango is a foreign fruit that came to India through Goa along with Alfonso De Albuquerque, the Portuguese explorer and military strategist who led many Portuguese invasions in 1600s. From Goa, the alphonso mango travelled to different places like Ratnagiri, other konkan region and southern side of India as well.
