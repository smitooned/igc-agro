History

The Alphonso mango is a foreign fruit that came to India through Goa along with Alfonso De Albuquerque, the Portuguese explorer and military strategist who led many Portuguese invasions in the 1600s. From Goa, the Alphonso mango travelled to different places like Ratnagiri, other Konkan region and southern side of India as well.

Why Devgad mangoes ?


Chemical Free

100% Organic

Carbide Free
Our mangoes are known across the world for its unique delicious taste, enticing aroma and beautiful yellow-orange hue. They are rich with nutrients, minerals and vitamins. Our superior quality has earned Geographical Identification (GI) Tag which is a hallmark of authenticity and quality of a product produced in Devgad region. To get these features, we use the traditional ripening process using grass haystack which is known as ‘Aadi’ in Devgad. We strictly prohibit the use of calcium carbonate to speed up the ripening process and for early harvest, thus making our mangoes “Carbide-Free”. We do not use artificial pesticides, chemical additives or synthetic fertilisers in our farms. We believe in organic farming.
